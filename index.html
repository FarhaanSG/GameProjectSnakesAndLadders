Instructions

Each player takes turns in rolling the dice and must roll a six in order to move their piece onto the board.

If a player rolls a six they are able to roll again until they do not land on a six.

Player roll one after another, unless they roll a six, and must travel down the snake if they piece land on a number with the snakes head and up the ladder if their piece lands on a number with the bottom of the ladder.

The first player to reach the 100th position is the WINNER!
